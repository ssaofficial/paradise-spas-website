What to ask when you get there

  • Which seat fits your body? Loungers, open seating, depth, and jet placement all feel different in person.
  • What is the real monthly payment? Ask about the model, delivery, tax, financing term, and what changes the payment.
  • Will it handle North Dakota winter? Ask what ownership looks like when it is 20 below and you still want 104 degree water.
  • How easy is it to maintain? You should know the real weekly routine before you pick a tub.
