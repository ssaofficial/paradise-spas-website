Template Operations

The page customers see should stay synced with the admin.

This is the fulfillment promise: dealer staff manage units once, and every public page, product page, and GHL tag follows the same source of truth.

1. Dealer updates admin

Staff adds price, monthly payment, status, quantity, promo label, quick facts, and image gallery in /admin.

2. Inventory API serves D1

GET /api/inventory returns only public records with status available, pending, or sold depending on page rules.

3. Lead routes with product tags

A product lead carries ghl_tags, URL, status, campaign, UTM values, and source channel into the lead pipeline.
