About This Spa

The Tranquility Harbor is designed for uncomplicated family use. Its open bench seating allows as many as seven people to sit together without dedicating a large section of the shell to a lounger. At 82 inches square, it provides family-capable room while remaining easier to place than the 92-inch models. Forty-two jets, dual pumps, a cascading waterfall, underwater lighting, padded headrests, ozone sanitation, RMAX insulation, and an insulated cover give it the core premium features buyers use and appreciate. It is a smart fit for a household that wants a comfortable shared spa rather than the highest jet count or the flashiest lighting package.

Best For

Families who want open seating, dependable premium features, and an easier-to-place 82-inch footprint.
